Topman Did It Again

08 Nov 17

Topman tapped into the color wheel for its 2017 fall/winter collection shown at London’s Fashion Week. Spanning the full range of hues from various shades of black to neons and pastels, the collection features a multitude of oversized cuts, which are now the norm. Offerings include a diverse selection of outerwear essentials for battling colder weather and the elements like coats, hoodies, long-sleeve shirts and suits while joggers, jeans and tracksuits round out the collection. The British fashion house manages to maintain a nice balance between pieces donning its logo versus animal patterns, florals and gingham.
