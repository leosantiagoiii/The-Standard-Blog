MATTI JÄ TEPPO: JVG

02 Nov 17

Breaking Records Is Their Hobby

JVG (also known as Jare & VilleGalle) from Finland started their humble days in 2011 with their debut album, "Mustaa Kultaa" which went number 1 in the Finnish Charts & was certified Gold within 3 weeks.

By 2012, they released jvg.fi (or JVG Finland/Suomi) which also went number 1. Two years after, Voitolla Yöhön was released and also got number 1. Fast forward to 2015 and they released 247365 which broke their number 1 streak. They peaked at 4 with lesses sales compared to their past years.

Earlier this year, the duo broke the record in Spotify for most streams, beating Cheek's a few years ago. This was the return of their rise - Popkorni. I have yet to review the album but I'm looking forward to giving them 7 & up out of 10.
